Our Heritage

Maruthi Suzuki has been at the forefront of the Indian automobile industry for over four decades. Established in 1981 as a joint venture between the Government of India and Suzuki Motor Corporation, we revolutionized the way India drives by making cars accessible and reliable.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional value to our customers by providing affordable, reliable, and fuel-efficient vehicles that meet the needs of every Indian family. We are committed to innovation, customer satisfaction, and sustainable mobility.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Widest range of cars in every price segment
  • Strong service and dealership network across India
  • Leading safety and emission standards
  • Over 25 million satisfied customers and counting

Innovation & Sustainability

From Smart Hybrid Technology to CNG options and electric future-readiness, Maruthi Suzuki is committed to creating eco-friendly and technology-driven vehicles for the future of mobility in India.

Global Presence

Maruthi Suzuki not only dominates the Indian market but also exports to over 100 countries, proudly representing Indian engineering and reliability on the global stage.
