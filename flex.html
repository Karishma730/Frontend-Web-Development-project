About VVIT

Vasireddy Venkatadri Institute of Technology (VVIT) is a premier engineering college located in Nambur, Guntur district, Andhra Pradesh.

Established in 2007, VVIT offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology, affiliated to JNTUK and approved by AICTE.

Academics and Departments

  • Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  • Information Technology (IT)
  • Mechanical and Civil Engineering
  • AI & Data Science, AI & Machine Learning

VVIT is known for its emphasis on quality education, modern infrastructure, strong industry connections, and active student participation in national and international events.
